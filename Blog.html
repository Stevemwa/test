Digital Safe

Mechatronics System Programming class group project on a Digital Safe with Atmega 32, featuring a 4x4 keypad, 2x12 LCD, relay for locking, dedicated "Open" button, and a tilt sensor for security. The tilt sensor activates a loud buzzer alarm if the safe is moved without proper authorization.

Control Access System

A secure clocking-in system: Fingerprint sensor, LCD display, and relay for employee attendance. It offers precise time tracking through fingerprint recognition. When an authorized print is scanned, the relay unlocks the door, ensuring secure access.

Traffi Ai

Leveraging cutting-edge smart vehicle number plates to gather vehicle movement data within a city. This data serves as the foundation for developing a machine learning model aimed at optimizing traffic light control. By reducing congestion and emissions, our initiative received recognition from the Institution of Engineers of Kenya (IEK) during the World Engineering Day 2023 at the University of Nairobi Towers.

Telemetric Dashcam

This printed circuit board (PCB) serves as a pivotal component in a sophisticated telemetric dashcam concept designed to enhance fleet tracking capabilities. The telemetric data generated by this system holds significant potential for application in the insurance industry. Specifically, insurance companies can leverage this data to establish more accurate risk profiles, leading to potential reductions in insurance premiums. Furthermore, the PCB functions as a critical element in an accident black box, enabling comprehensive recording and analysis of vehicular incidents.
